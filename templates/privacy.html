Privacy Policy

Your trust is everything—guerilla-style transparency, no BS.

1. Who We Are

Gorilla Camping ("we", "us", "our") is a blog and resource for off-grid, guerilla-style living and affordable camping gear. Our site address is https://gorillacamping.site.

2. What Data We Collect

  • Email: If you sign up for our newsletter, we store your email securely (and never sell it).
  • Analytics: We use Google Analytics, Microsoft Clarity, and Facebook Pixel to understand site usage and optimize content/ads. All data is anonymized.
  • Cookies: We use cookies for analytics, affiliate tracking, and consent pop-ups (CookieYes).
  • Affiliate Links: Clicking product links may use tracking cookies so we get credit for any sale (helps keep the site running).

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To send you the newsletter (if you opted in)
  • To optimize the site for a better guerilla experience
  • To track affiliate sales (so we can earn commission)
  • We NEVER sell your data. Period.

4. Third-Party Services

  • MailerLite: Handles email marketing (privacy: see MailerLite privacy)
  • Google Analytics: For stats and marketing (privacy: see Google privacy)
  • Microsoft Clarity: For site improvement (privacy: see Microsoft privacy)
  • Amazon Associates: For affiliate earnings (privacy: see Amazon privacy)

5. Your Rights

  • Unsubscribe anytime using the link in any email.
  • Contact us here to request data deletion or updates.

6. Children’s Privacy

This site is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Policy Updates
